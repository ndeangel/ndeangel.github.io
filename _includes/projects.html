SMILE

SMILE is an international graduate-student-led space mission with a team of mostly doctoral students. The goal of our mission is to land a small (CubeSat-sized) spacecraft that conducts meaningful science on the surface of the moon by 2024. The objective of the primary payloads is to perform in-situ measurements of the radiation environment on the lunar surface and to characterize the behavior of radiation resistant biological organisms for future manned lunar exploration.
